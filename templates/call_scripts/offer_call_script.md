# Offer Call Script

**Purpose:** To share the offer clearly, warmly, and confidently, while giving the candidate space to respond.

---

**Hi {{first_name}}, it’s {{manager_name}} calling from {{team_name}}. Is now still a good time for a quick chat?**

Great — thank you.

I’m really pleased to say that we’d love to offer you the **{{role_title}}** role. Throughout the process, it was clear how much thought you bring to your work and how naturally your strengths align with what the team is building. We’re genuinely excited about the perspective you’d bring.

Here’s a quick overview of the offer:

- **Role:** {{role_title}}  
- **Team:** {{team_name}}  
- **Start Date:** {{start_date}}  
- **Working Pattern:** {{working_pattern}}  
- **Salary:** {{salary_details}}  
- **Contract Type:** {{contract_type}}  

You’ll receive the full offer pack shortly, which will include your contract and all the details you’ll need to review. There’s absolutely no pressure to make a decision on the spot — I want you to have the space to think things through properly.

How are you feeling about everything so far?

**Pause — give them time to respond.**

If you have any questions, big or small, I’m more than happy to talk things through. And if anything comes up after the call, just reach out — I’m here to help.

Really looking forward to the possibility of welcoming you to the team.

**End the call warmly and without rushing.**
