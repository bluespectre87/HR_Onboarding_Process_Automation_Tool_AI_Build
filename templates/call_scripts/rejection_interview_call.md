# Interview Stage — Rejection Call Script

**Purpose:** To close the loop respectfully after interviews, acknowledging the candidate’s effort and giving space for optional feedback.

---

**Hi {{first_name}}, it’s {{manager_name}} calling from {{team_name}}. Is now still a good time for a quick chat?**

Great — thank you.

I wanted to follow up after your interview for the **{{role_title}}** role. It was genuinely great to meet you and hear more about your experience, your approach to the work, and what you’re looking for next.

After completing all interviews, we’ve made the difficult decision not to move forward. This was a close call — the final decision came down to specific experience that aligned more directly with what the team needs right now.

I know this isn’t the outcome you were hoping for, and I want to acknowledge the time, preparation, and thought you put into the process. It really does come through, and we appreciated it.

If you’d find it helpful, I’m more than happy to share some feedback
