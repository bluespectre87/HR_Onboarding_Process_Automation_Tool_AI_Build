# Screening Call — Rejection Script

**Purpose:** To close the loop respectfully after a screening call, giving clarity without overwhelming detail.

---

**Hi {{first_name}}, it’s {{manager_name}} calling from {{team_name}}. Is now still a good time for a quick chat?**

Great — thank you.

I wanted to follow up after our screening conversation for the **{{role_title}}** role. I really appreciated the chance to learn more about your experience and what you’re looking for next.

After reviewing the full shortlist, we’ve decided not to progress your application to the next stage. This was a competitive round, and the decision came down to specific experience that aligned more closely with what the team needs right now.

I know this isn’t the outcome you were hoping for, and I want to acknowledge the time and openness you brought to the process — it genuinely matters.

If you’d find it helpful, I’m more than happy to share a bit more context or offer some feedback. Just let me know what would be most useful for you.
